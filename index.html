Depeche Mode - the Band

Depeche Mode was formed in 1980 in Basildon, Essex by Dave Gahan, Martn Gore, Andy Clarke and Vince Clarke. Later on Vince Clarke left the band shortly after it's conception and Alan Wilder joined in 1982. Wilder left the band in 1995. Andy Fletcher sadly passed away on 26 May 2022.
